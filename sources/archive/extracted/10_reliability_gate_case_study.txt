1     Strategy Overview
SEP trades strictly on structural recurrence: minute-level OHLCV candles are embedded into
the quantum feature manifold to produce coherence (c), stability (s), entropy (H), rupture
density (ρ), and hazard (λ). A trade is eligible only when the current signature has repeated
at least three times in the past hour and the hazard remains below the calibrated cap defined
in config/echo strategy.yaml. PortfolioManager sizes each admission at approximately 1%
of account NAV while enforcing spread and margin guards.


2     System Pipeline
Step 1: Ingestion – OANDA REST API delivers validated M1 candles into Valkey keys of
        the form md:candles:{instrument}:M1.

Step 2: Fingerprinting – The manifold builder computes the dynamic metrics and persists
        signals under sep:signal:*.

Step 3: Gate Evaluation – scripts/rolling backtest evaluator.py checks repetition counts,
        hazard, and session boundaries before publishing gate:last:{instrument}.

Step 4: Execution – scripts/trading/portfolio manager.py consumes gate payloads, en-
        sures guard compliance, and submits orders to OANDA with position size ≈ 507.66 / 100.

Step 5: Monitoring – Telemetry is surfaced via Prometheus metrics, risk:alloc budget,
        and the web dashboard for manual verification.

The gate payload at the moment of entry reported a repetition count of 1, hazard λ = 0.118110,
and cap λmax = 0.118110. The signature bucket was c=0.460, s=0.882. During this session,
NZD USD was running under a single-observation override while hazard quantiles were being
recalibrated, so the minimum repetition requirement was 1 and satisfied. With the gate admitted
and the Sydney window open, PortfolioManager sized the BUY order to 2147 (buy) units.

4     Discussion
This case study demonstrates that the reliability-gated pipeline admitted an instrument only
when the microstructure repeated with low hazard, sized the position deterministically from
NAV, and captured profits without manual intervention. The artefacts generated alongside this
document (metadata JSON, plot, gate payload) provide auditable evidence of each step.
